The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • a butter knife
  • a sharp knife
  • a cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both sides of the bread next to each other on the cutting board
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side
  3. On the right side spread a 1/8 inch of jelly
  4. carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut in half
  6. Slowly, and elegantly place in mouth and enjoy the delectable meal that you've just constructed
